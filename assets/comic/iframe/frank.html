Progress Reports

Report number 1

After discussing plans with my group, we discussed several characters we wanted to implement. After creating a list, we decided on some which we wanted to be considered main characters. I drew sketches of each of these main characters, which can be seen in the slides presentation we created. Some of these characters' designs are inspired by other characters, which I will outline now. The character Rick's design is based off the character Hajime Hinata from the video game Super Danganronpa 2: Goodby Despair. His design is based off the simplicity and plainness of Hajime's, but both still keep complex images on his clothing. The character design for Jerry is based off the protagonist of the video game Persona 3. I only really took the hair from this design, purely because I like the way it looks. Another design I used was that of Rhandall's, who is based off of Kamina from Gainax Studio's Gurren Lagann. The only part of this design I really used for Rhandall is the glasses and the hair, again because I like the aesthetic of it. The final design that derives from another is that of Andy, or "Tezuka." His design is a mix of two, those of Gundham Tanaka of Super Danganronpa 2: Goodby Despair and that of Kakashi Hatake from the manga series Naruto. The primary ideas I took from Kakashi's were his mask and gloves, which are both used to make him look like a ninja. From Gundham, I used a similar design which shows someone trying to look like a ninja but you can still tell that they are not one, as reflected by both of them wearing normal clothing underneath any gear they may be wearing. Veronica's design is completely original, and not inspired by any other characters I know of.
